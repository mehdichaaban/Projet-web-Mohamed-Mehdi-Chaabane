Sustainable Minds Turned Into Business

In 2016, ICE CM started as a producer of green energy and hot water from our home base. More than five years later, our mission remains the same: adding value to the world. We are committed to running our company for the greater good—a sustainable world for future generations.

With this in mind, the next step to becoming a producer for the circular economy was taken, especially when we found a sustainable destination for the residual heat from our plant.
